Plov

Description

The national dish of many Central Asian nations

Ingredients

  • Lamb
  • beef
  • carrots
  • onions
  • garlic head

Steps to cook!

  1. Trim beef
  2. Preheat your dutch oven
  3. Heat to medium
  4. Add 3/4 cups hot water
  5. cover low heat for 45 min
  6. Rinse rice
  7. Spread rice and add 4 cups hot water
  8. Garlic
  9. Poke 7-10 holes and cook for 15 min
